These delicious keto brownies are easy to make and will satisfy your sweet tooth! This recipe makes 8 servings. Each serving contains only 44 calories!

These rich and delicious keto brownies are a great treat to help you stay on your low carb eating plan. They can also be made in cupcake or mini cupcake tims and topped with keto frosting!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup oat fiber
  • 1/2 cup Swerve powdered sweetner
  • 1/2 cup Allulose
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp unflavored gelatin
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup plain greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Spray an 8x8 pan with cooking spray.
  3. Mix dry ingredients.
  4. Add egg and yogurt. Slowly add almond milk and mix well.
  5. Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for 20-25 minutes.
  6. Brownies will be done when they are firm in the middle.
  7. Enjoy!
