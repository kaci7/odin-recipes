Looking for a fast and easy keto breakfast? Try these delicious keto cinnamon rolls!

This recipe makes 4 small cinnamon rolls! Perfect for breakfast or brunch!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Carbquick (and a little extra to roll out with)
  • 1/4-1/3 cup plain greek yogurt
  • 1/2 Tbsp butter
  • 2 Tbsp granular Swerve
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 Tbsp unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup powdered Swerve

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 390 degrees.
  2. Spread out a piece of parchment paper or silpat mat on a baking sheet.
  3. Mix Carbquick and yogurt to form a soft dough. Add yogurt a little at a time. Dough should be firm enough to roll out. .
  4. Roll out into a rectangle about 5x8 inches. Spread dough with melted butter and then sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar. Roll up to form a 5 inch roll and cut into 4 pieces. Place on baking sheet. Bake for 10 minutes or until lightly brown on the edges.
  5. While the rolls are cooking, mix the powdered sugar and almond milk to form a glaze. Add more sugar or almond milk until to forms a spreadable consistency..
  6. After taking cinnamon rolls out of the oven, spread glaze on each roll.
  7. Enjoy!
